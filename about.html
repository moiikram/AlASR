About Al ASR

Al ASR is a community dedicated to understanding and living by the profound teachings of Surah Al-Asr, the 103rd chapter of the Quran. This short yet powerful surah emphasizes the importance of time, faith, righteous deeds, and mutual encouragement toward truth and patience.

Our organization was founded with the belief that these principles can transform lives and communities. We aim to spread awareness and inspire action based on the timeless wisdom contained in these verses.

Our Mission

  • To educate people about the meaning and significance of Surah Al-Asr.
  • To promote a lifestyle rooted in faith and good deeds.
  • To foster a supportive community that encourages truth and perseverance.
  • To provide resources and insights that make these teachings accessible to all.

Our Vision

We envision a world where individuals value their time, uphold their faith, and work together for the betterment of humanity, as inspired by Surah Al-Asr: "By time, indeed, mankind is in loss, except for those who have believed and done righteous deeds and advised each other to truth and advised each other to patience."
